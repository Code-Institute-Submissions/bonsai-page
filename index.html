About


Bonsai (Japanese: 盆栽) is the art of growing ornamental, artificially dwarfed varieties of trees and shrubs in pots.


This webpage was created to get people inspired to start with bonsai and to showcase some of my personal collection. The bonsai featured on this website are less than 25cm in height, also known as Shohin trees. All trees on this website origin from Japan and the pots were made by Terahata Satomi.


You can learn more on bonsai by visiting Bonsai Empire website.
